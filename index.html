🐾 Welcome! 🐾

This website celebrates the creativity of the furry fandom— a community built around art, storytelling, animation, games, and anthropomorphic animal characters.

What is the furry fandom?

The furry fandom is a creative community focused on characters that combine human and animal traits. People participate in many different ways: drawing artwork, writing stories, role-playing, making costumes, creating games, or simply appreciating the creativity.

Meet Our Fictional Mascot

Nova the fox is a fictional explorer who loves coding, video games, music, and meeting new friends. Nova appears throughout this demo website to show how an original furry character might be presented.

A Brief History

The modern furry fandom grew during the 1980s, expanding through conventions, online communities, artists, writers, and game developers. Today it includes people from many different backgrounds who enjoy creative projects involving anthropomorphic characters.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is everyone in the fandom the same?

No. People enjoy different parts of the hobby, from drawing and writing to gaming or collecting artwork.

Do you need a fursona?

No. Some people create one, while others simply enjoy the art and community.
