What is a furry?

A furry is someone who enjoys anthropomorphic animal characters— animals with human-like personalities or traits. Many people enjoy furry art, stories, games, costumes, or creating their own original characters called fursonas.

Why I Like It

  • 🐺 I enjoy creative character designs.
  • 🎨 I like the artwork and creativity.
  • 📖 I enjoy stories featuring animal characters.
  • 🎮 Many games and animations include fun anthropomorphic characters.

Fun Facts

  • Everyone enjoys the hobby in their own way.
  • Some people draw.
  • Some write stories.
  • Some make costumes called fursuits.
  • Others simply enjoy the community and artwork.
